PERSONAL DETAILS

Mandlonke Mkhiwane is a South African male model. He is from the Eastern Cape (Flagstaff) but he is currently residing in Butterworth (Egcuwa) due to his furthering of his studies at Walter Sisulu University (WSU) Ibika Campus.

Mandlonke has completed a National Diploma (System development) I.T and has completed his NQF Level 4 in Primary Agriculture. He is a professional model; his titles that he has been crowned withi as a model are as follows (2015 Mr. KZN TOP MODEL, 2016 Mr Gamalakhe, 2016 Mr Lusikisiki, 2017 Mr South Africa Finalist and Mr Tourism SA 1st prince) and won Model of the year in2017 RYOSA AWORDS

. He is a very intrinsically enthusiastic person that is able to figure out how diverse people can work together inventively. He is a very committed leader; he leads as a Coordinator of Phumlani M foundation Butterworth Region in 2018 n 2019 and Organizer of I.T Society in 2017 at WSU Butterworth campus.

Mandlonke grew up in a small rural village called Kwakhanyayo in Lusikisiki in the Eastern Cape province in South Africa and he went to public schools. He aspires to inspire and groom the youth to do better. He is currently nominated at MISTER AFRICA INTERNATIONAL 2019 he is going to represent South Africa. The competition has not yet confirmed where is it going to be held.

If you are willing to sponsor him you can reach him at mandlonkemkiwane@gamil.com. In 5 years from now he hopes to use his knowledge to build and inspire other African people. When he goes out into the real world, the people he would like to target is the African Youth, as he would like to be a leader in the Modeling industry, the reason for this is because he has learned so much from working with them and he is where he is because of them.
